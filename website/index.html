FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about DetSEG and how it fits into your practice.

DetSEG accepts standard colonoscopy images in JPG, PNG, and JPEG formats, as well as video files in MP4 and AVI formats. The system automatically handles resizing for optimal processing.

Our YOLOv8-based detection achieves ~95% mAP@50 on benchmark datasets. The U-Net segmentation module achieves a mean Dice coefficient of 0.89 and IoU of 0.84 for precise boundary delineation.

Absolutely. DetSEG runs entirely on your local infrastructure. No images or data are transmitted to external servers. All AI inference happens on-premise.

No — and that's by design. DetSEG is a CADe tool intended to assist clinicians as a "second pair of eyes" to reduce polyp miss rates (6–27%) and improve adenoma detection rates.

Any modern computer works. For real-time performance, an NVIDIA GPU (RTX series) is recommended. CPU-only mode is supported for image-by-image analysis.

Stage 1 uses YOLOv8 to rapidly scan and localize candidate polyp regions. Stage 2 crops each detection and feeds it through a U-Net (ResNet-34 encoder) for pixel-level segmentation. This "detect-then-segment" approach reduces false positives.
